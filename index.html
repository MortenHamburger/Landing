About

I am a Danish national who first found interest in the bricklaying field, when travelling abroad on a working holiday in Australia. After my experience in Australia I decided to move to Leicester to continue my passion for bricklaying and have since been working with bricklaying all around the UK. Currently I am situated in Brighton and do most of my work in and around the Brighton area.

Qualifications

I have completed my education and I am certified bricklayer. Living abroad for the past 7 years have also greatly improved my linguistic skills which have made me fluent in English and Danish. Furthermore I speak and understand some Spanish and German, which also helps communicating with international co-workers as well as clients
